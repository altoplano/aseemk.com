Welcome

You've reached the site of Aseem Kishore, a software engineer at Microsoft and recent alumnus of MIT.

At Microsoft, I work in the Seadragon group within Live Labs. My major contributions have been Seadragon Ajax, a Javascript implementation of our group's zooming technology, and Seadragon.com, a platform and service for viewing and creating Seadragon content. Before working at Microsoft, I interned at Intuit within what was formerly called the Innovation Lab. There, I designed and developed Thumbstrips, a Firefox extension to track your browsing session visually. You can learn more about all of these via my software page.

At MIT, I majored in course 6-3, meaning mostly the CS part of EECS. During my senior year, I taught 6.189, which has been permanently saved into OCW, and TA'ed 6.005. Outside of my major, I played in the FJE, led the Mock Trial team and competed with the Sport TKD team. Since graduating, I've returned on behalf of Microsoft for career fairs and to help judge 6.470. You can learn more about my MIT career through my academia page.
